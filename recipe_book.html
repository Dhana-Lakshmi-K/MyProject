Recipe Name: Masala Chai

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 teaspoons tea leaves
  • 2 teaspoons sugar (or to taste)
  • 1-inch piece of ginger (grated)
  • 2-3 cardamom pods

Instructions:

  1. Boil water and add ginger and cardamom.
  2. Add tea leaves and simmer for 2 minutes.
  3. Pour milk and sugar, and bring it to a boil.
  4. Strain and serve hot.

Recipe Name: Vegetable Biryani

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 2 tablespoons ghee or oil
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • Mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, beans)
  • 1 teaspoon biryani masala
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Wash and cook basmati rice, and keep aside.
  2. Heat ghee and sauté onions until golden.
  3. Add mixed vegetables and biryani masala, cook until tender.
  4. Mix cooked rice with the vegetable mixture and garnish with coriander.
  5. Serve with raita or pickle.

Recipe Name: Palak Paneer

Ingredients:

  • 200g paneer (cubed)
  • 2 cups spinach (blanched and pureed)
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt and cream (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil and sauté onions until golden.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and tomato puree, and cook until thickened.
  3. Mix in spinach puree and spices.
  4. Add paneer cubes and simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Garnish with cream and serve hot with naan or rice.
